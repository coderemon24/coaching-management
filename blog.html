How to Prepare for HSC Board in 90 Days

By Academic Team | 25 Feb, 2026 | 6 min read

Start with chapter prioritization: High-frequency chapters first. Create a weekly cycle of concept revision, timed practice, and error notebook review. Avoid random studying; follow a fixed exam-oriented plan. প্রতি সপ্তাহে অন্তত ১টি full mock দিন এবং ভুলের তালিকা maintain করুন।

Suggested 3-Part Routine

  • - Morning: Concept revision (90 minutes)
  • - Evening: Problem-solving drill (120 minutes)
  • - Night: Mistake log + quick recap (30 minutes)
