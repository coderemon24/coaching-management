How to Prepare for HSC Board in 90 Days

By Academic Team | 25 Feb, 2026

Build a 90-day plan in three phases: foundation revision, intensive practice, and final mock tuning. Every week must include concept refresh, timed solving, and mistake review. Random study destroys consistency.

Phase Plan

  • - Days 1-30: Complete core chapter revision
  • - Days 31-65: Daily problem-solving drills
  • - Days 66-90: Full mocks + weak topic fixing

Use a mistake notebook to track repeated errors. প্রতি রবিবার নিজের mock performance review করুন।
